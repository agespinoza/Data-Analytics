## ‚õèÔ∏è Mining Engineering
Mining generates massive telemetry data where analytics saves lives and reduces operational costs.
* **Use Case:** **Predictive Maintenance on Haul Trucks.** Analyzing oil temperature and vibration to prevent engine failure.
* **Use Case:** **Fragmentation Analysis.** Using image recognition to optimize the crushing and grinding process.
* **Key Reference:** * *Data Analytics Applied to the Mining Industry* by **Ali Soofastaei**. This is a fundamental guide on using AI and Big Data to improve productivity and safety in mining operations.
* **Public Data Source:** [USGS Mineral Resources Program](https://www.usgs.gov/centers/geologic-and-environmental-collectors-resource-center) - Technical data on mineral deposits and global production.

## üèéÔ∏è Vehicle Inspection & IoT
In the inspection industry, data ensures environmental compliance and road safety.
* **Use Case:** **Anomaly Detection in Emissions.** Identifying fraudulent tests by analyzing sensor patterns that deviate from engine profiles.
* **Use Case:** **Equipment Calibration Trends.** Predicting when gas analyzers or dynamometers will require maintenance.
* **Key Reference:**
    * *Hands-On Industrial Internet of Things* by **Giacomo Veneri**. Excellent for understanding how to process data from sensors and industrial machines.
* **Public Data Source:** [NASA Open Data Portal](https://data.nasa.gov/) - Access to over 30,000 datasets, including Earth science, aeronautics, and space exploration missions.

## üõ∞Ô∏è Aerospace Engineering
In aerospace, data analytics is the difference between a successful mission and a catastrophic failure.
* **Use Case:** **Structural Health Monitoring (SHM).** Using strain gauge data to detect fatigue in aircraft components.
* **Use Case:** **Flight Data Monitoring (FDM).** Analyzing "Black Box" data to identify deviations from standard operating procedures.
* **Key Reference:**
    * *Data Science for Nano-scale Surface Engineering* by **G.W. Critchlow** (or specialized journals like *Progress in Aerospace Sciences*).
    * *Data Science for Transport* by **Charles Fox**, which covers analytical methods applicable to complex transit and flight systems.
